Premier League Statistical Leaders 2020-2021

Statistical comparisions for the 2020-2021 Premier League Season.

This project is intended to visualize the Premier League for the 2020-2021 season. The first dropdown shows the top 10 players by different categories. From there, you can compare two players to see who had the better season. Finally, we have a map showing country of origin for all players that season.
